FIGURE 3. Dual-pathway model of interoception and mental health.
===============================================================

Panel A: Path coefficients (standardized) for the dual-pathway SEM.
Panel B: SEM diagram showing IAS and IATS effects on TAS and Somatic pathways,
and downstream effects on mental health.

Panel C: Pathway dominance by profile. Bars show the percentage of the IAS
indirect effect through TAS vs Somatic routes with bootstrap 95% CIs.

Panels D and E: Simple slopes illustrating IAS effects by profile. Panel D
(Somatic) shows profile-dependent slopes (moderation), whereas Panel E (TAS)
shows consistent negative slopes across profiles (no moderation).

Sample: N = 833. Bootstrap = 5000.
