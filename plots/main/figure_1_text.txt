FIGURE 1. Interoceptive profile clustering and outcomes.
=======================================================

Panel A: Correlation heatmap (lower triangle) for IATS, IAS, TAS, SSS-8, PCS,
PHQ-9, GAD-7, and STAI. Cell colors encode sign and significance; values are
Pearson r. This panel provides the variable-level context used for clustering
and outcome contrasts.

Panel B: Scatter of IAS vs IATS with 90% ellipses for the 3 k-means profiles
(Efficient, Hypervigilant, Uncertain). Colors match profile labels across the
figure.

Panels C-H: Outcome means by profile with SE bars and Tukey HSD markers.
TAS (C), SSS-8 (D), PCS (E), PHQ-9 (F), GAD-7 (G), and STAI (H). These panels
show how the profiles differ across alexithymia, somatic symptoms, pain,
depression, anxiety, and trait anxiety.

Sample: N = 833.
